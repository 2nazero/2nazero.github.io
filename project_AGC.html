AGE Project

The AGE project focuses on supporting policy decision-making using AI to analyze large-scale government data. This project was conducted as part of the 2023 AI Grand Challenge (Phase 2), organized by the Ministry of Science and ICT.

Key Contributions

  • Developed AI models to efficiently extract and generate policy-relevant information from government data.
  • Participated as a 2-member dataset creation team, focusing on text data parsing for AI Hub challenges.
  • Implemented LDA models for topic modeling.
  • Used MiniBatchKMeans for clustering datasets effectively.
  • Utilized the KLUE-YNAT model to generate relevant titles for datasets.
  • Achieved success with a 7th place ranking among participants.

Research Process

The project involved the following steps:

  1. Preprocessed large-scale government datasets for training and validation.
  2. Used advanced models for topic extraction, clustering, and title generation.
  3. Iterated on models to optimize outputs for policy decision-making purposes.

Conclusion
