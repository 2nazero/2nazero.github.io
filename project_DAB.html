DAB (Data Analytics for Business) Contest

Parsed risk area and optimized routes in Seoul’s Songpa district navigation system
Integrated TTS/STT voice functions for real-time guidance.

Project Overview

Through the DAB (Data Analytics for Business) Contest, I gained comprehensive experience in the entire lifecycle of data utilization. This included parsing risk areas, deriving optimal routes, and integrating TTS/STT voice functions. The project demonstrated my ability to design and implement the full data process in service environments—from data collection and preprocessing to analysis, visualization, insight derivation, and decision-making.

Key Features

  • Parsed risk areas using SQL to identify significant causes (e.g., higher risks during U-turns).
  • Assigned risk scores to areas and designed routes that avoided high-risk zones.
  • Integrated TTS (Text-to-Speech) functions to alert users in extremely risky zones.
  • Utilized tools like Amplitude, BigQuery, and Superset for customer journey tracking, cohort analysis, and real-time monitoring.

Technologies Used

SQL, Python, Amplitude, BigQuery, Superset, F-Test Analysis

Key Achievements

Created a navigation system that improved safety by highlighting risk-prone areas and offering optimized routes. Enabled decision-making at an organizational level by presenting intuitive dashboards for monitoring and analyzing objectives.
